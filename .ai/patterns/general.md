## Notes

- Use numbered H2 steps (`## Step 1:`, `## Step 2:`) for sequential tutorials
- Screenshots go in `docs/deploy-applications/images/` and are referenced with relative paths: `![Alt text](./images/screenshot.png)`
- No tabs needed unless showing alternative approaches
- Sidebar placement is under "Developers" category (not "Traefik Ingress")
